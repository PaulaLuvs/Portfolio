About Paula Guevara

Hi! I'm a rising junior at St. John's Prep in Astoria, Queens.
I am 15 years old and my birthday is in October.
I started Girls Who Code because my Science Olympiad director would not allow me to join GameOn, an event where a competitor creates a video game in under 40 minutes.
Although I was willing to learn, he decided that a group of freshmen boys would be better suited because "boys are naturally more interested in code."


I'm here to prove him wrong and become the first female member in our school's GameOn team.

My interests include:

  • Painting/Drawing
  • Science (Microbiology and Genetics, in particular)
  • Singing
